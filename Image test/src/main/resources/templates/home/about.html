About

Mary Jo Ann Torres is the woman behind Line art by Jo Torres. Mary Jo has always had a great passion for drawing, but never really believed that her drawings could be enjoyable to others than herself. Although she has been drawing for a long time, it has not always been in the form of line art. Mary Jo has tried many different styles of art before she finally fell in love with the line art style. In the beginning she kept her art to herself, but she has gradually become more comfortable with the idea that others should be a part of her art journey.
In 2017, Mary Jo decided to make an Instagram, so she could share her art, not only with her friends and family, but also with the rest of the world. People around her slowly started to notice her art. Many has bought artworks from her, and people has also gotten her drawings tattooed on their bodies.
